Dashclicker

Dashclicker is an open-source free-to-play incremental clicker game. This is the second iteration of the game, rewritten from scratch in Angular. Initial version of the game is still available for play at old.dashclicker.com.

Frequently asked questions

I am stuck, next upgrades are unreachable

If you can't reach a task milestone, try stocking up on clicks & analytics and using the Restart game button you'll find in your wallet. Due to the way of how costs and prices are calculated, this is the actual way of how to progress in the game (prestige mechanic). While tasks reset costs, calculations for the next upgrade costs take into account your already owned number of upgrades, thus you should upgrade as far as possible before using resets from tasks.

How long does it take to complete the game?

It should take you around 2-4 hours to complete the game (all tasks finished, level #50 reached), but as new features are added, the play time might increase considerably.

There is too much clicking, there is no Buy max button

If you are playing on a computer, you can click on a button and hold Enter key to trigger the action repeatedly. You can also navigate between buttons by pressing Tab (forwards) or Shift-Tab (backwards).

How does the wallet work?

Values listed in bold are the actual values stored in the wallet, while the values after slash / are maximal values the wallet has ever held. If you want to keep your assets between different sessions / devices, deposit into your wallet before exiting the game.

I started the game as a guest but now want to create an account

You can transfer assets between accounts. While logged into your guest account: 1) withdraw from wallet (note that it is not possible to withdraw boosts) 2) log out 3) register an account 4) deposit into wallet.

I logged in and found my wallet empty

Most likely you didn't deposit your assets before exiting the game, which can be considered a hard reset of the game. If you want to restore your assets, write an e-mail to dashclicker@dashclicker.com and I will reset your wallet to the max values it had in it.
